What is this?

JavaSound's MIDI API is intentionally low level. Its concepts are not what musicians are used to. They think Notes and beats, not events and ticks. This helps, I hope.

[top]

Why did you write another Java Music library?

Another? There was no other Java Music Library when I started this.

[top]

So, what's a Rockhopper?

Rockhopper penguins are the coolest birds on the planet. So, I like penguins. Got a problem with that?

[top]

Do I need a Score? I want to create just a track.

Knock yourself out. You can create a MIDITrack directly and play it. You just can't write it to a standard MIDI file. To do that, you do need a Score. That's not especially hard to do.
